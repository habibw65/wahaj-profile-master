Hello There!

I am a Geospatial Data Scientist with 5+ years of experience applying machine learning to satellite imagery for land cover, flood forecasting, GHG estimation, and ecological monitoring. I specialize in geospatial data pipelines, remote sensing analytics, and national-scale land use modeling.

Currently working as a Postdoctoral Researcher at Trinity College Dublin, I'm leading the development of a high-resolution satellite-driven model (ICONEEx) to upscale Net Ecosystem Exchange (NEE) of CO₂ across 92% of Ireland's terrestrial ecosystems. My research integrates cutting-edge remote sensing technologies with machine learning to support climate monitoring and environmental management.
